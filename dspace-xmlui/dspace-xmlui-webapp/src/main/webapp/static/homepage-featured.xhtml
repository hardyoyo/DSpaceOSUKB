Digital Storytelling

A digital story is a short movie which uses images, voice, and music to tell a story. The OSU Digital Storytelling Program helps the academic community communicate their passion for teaching, research, and outreach through personal, engaging storytelling. The Digital Stories in the KB are contributed by OSU faculty, staff, and students, as well as community members.
